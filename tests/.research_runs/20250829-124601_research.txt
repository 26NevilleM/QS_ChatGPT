When comparing Zendesk and Intercom for a support chat solution with 15 agents, Zendesk typically offers stronger SLAs and deeper reporting capabilities, making it ideal for analytics-focused teams. Intercom provides greater workflow flexibility and is often perceived as having a more user-friendly interface, but may involve higher total costs depending on feature requirements and usage. Rollout effort is generally comparable between the two, though individual team needs may influence implementation complexity.
