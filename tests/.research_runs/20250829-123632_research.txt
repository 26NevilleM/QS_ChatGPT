Storing customer support chat logs for 24 months under GDPR and UK GDPR requires a lawful basis, typically legitimate interests or consent, along with clear retention policies that justify the duration. Data Subject Rights (DSR) handling must ensure individuals can access, rectify, or delete their data. Additionally, a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) may be necessary if data processing poses a high risk to individual privacy, especially considering the sensitivity of the data involved.
